About

The company started operating on 15th of December 2010.The company had started selling motor spareparts then after getting more capital they start importing vehicles for sell.In 2014 the company started assembling its own cars.The main offices of the company are located along Mombasa road Near Syokimau Railway station.

The machinery that we provide is geniune because we import the best quality of the spare parts and we have qualifed staff who assemle the parts.Furthermore we have good assembling machines.We assemble upto 50-60 cars in a month.

We provide servicing to our customers at a cheaper fee as so long as you prove you had bought a vehicle from our Company
We are constantly upgrading our branches and also opening new branches across the country
As we provide machinery of construction and agriculture, we also accept tenders from the government where we build especially roads.
